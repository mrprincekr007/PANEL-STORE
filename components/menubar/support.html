Frequently Asked

Quick answers to common questions

How do I reset my HWID?
Go to the HWID Reset page from the menu, paste your new hardware identifier, and click Update HWID. Your HWID will be updated instantly.
How does the referral system work?
Share your unique referral link with friends. When they register using your link, you earn a commission on their first purchase.
How long does deposit take?
UPI Auto deposits are instant. Manual QR deposits are processed within 5-30 minutes after payment confirmation. Crypto deposits may take 10-30 minutes depending on network.
I didn't receive my purchase key
Check your purchase history on the Profile page. If the key is missing, contact support via email or Telegram with your transaction ID.
